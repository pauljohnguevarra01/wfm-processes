Plotting Offline Activities

To maintain productivity and ensure accurate reporting, all non-queue activities like coaching, meetings, and training must be scheduled in Assemble.

1. Weekly Verification

Team Leads check weekly to ensure their team's activities are plotted.

2. Request Submission

If segments are missing, the Team Lead formally requests the WFM team to plot the activity.

3. Real-Time Exception

For urgent activities, Team Leads must notify the Real-Time Analyst (RTA) at least 30 minutes in advance.

Schedule Swap & Change Requests

To ensure fairness and operational stability, all schedule modifications follow a strict set of guidelines.

  • Requests must be sent 2 weeks in advance.
  • Agents involved must have the same skill set.
  • Break/lunch swaps are only permitted for medical reasons with documentation.
  • Email subject must be: Program SWAP/SCHEDULE Request.
  • Note: Schedules are locked every Thursday at 2:00 AM MNL.

Paid Time Off (PTO) Requests

All PTO requests must be submitted through the official channels within the specified timelines.

  • File via Program Leave Form at least 2 weeks in advance.
  • Validation deadline is Thursday, 10:00 PM MNL.
  • The Leave Form must be fully completed.
  • Vacation Leaves cannot be swapped.

Suspension tickets will be rejected if required approvals are missing, documents are not attached, or the submission is past the deadline.
