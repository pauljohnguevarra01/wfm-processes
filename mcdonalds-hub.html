Schedule Swap & Change Requests

To ensure fairness and operational stability, all schedule modifications follow a strict set of guidelines.

  • Requests must be sent 2 weeks in advance.
  • Agents involved must have the same skill set.
  • Break/lunch swaps are only permitted for medical reasons with documentation.
  • Email subject must be: Program SWAP/SCHEDULE Request.
  • Note: Schedules are locked every Friday at 2:00 AM MNL (Thursday Shift). No changes are accepted after this deadline for the upcoming week.

PTO Request Process

1. Agent Informs Team Lead

The agent verbally informs their Team Lead of their intention to file for Paid Time Off.

2. Team Lead Adds to Tracker

The Team Lead adds the agent's request to their official PTO tracker for documentation.

3. Operations Manager Approves

The tracker is reviewed by the Operations Manager, Miles, who provides the final approval for all PTO requests.

4. WFM Plots Approved Leaves

Every week, our WFM team plots all approved leaves in IEX before the end of the Thursday shift, ensuring the master schedule is accurate.

Schedule Suspension Protocol

Schedule suspensions are required for extended absences. The process varies significantly based on the reason for the leave.

RTWO

3 Unauthorized Absences

Approver: OM/OD/HR

Requirement: NOD & Projected Attrition date. Ticket due by Thursday, 10:00 PM MNL.

Medical Leave (MLOA)

Validated Medical Certificate

Approver: OM or OD

Requirement: Clinic-validated MedCert for min. 3 days.

Maternity Leave

Maternity

Approver: OM/OD/HR

Requirement: MedCert with HR approval & completed forms.

Paternity Leave

Married Employees Only

Approver: OM/OD/HR

Requirement: Supporting documents provided to HR.

Termination

Attrition/Resignation

Approver: OM/OD/HR

Requirement: HR acknowledgement and SOM/OD sign-off.

Solo-Parent Leave

DSWD ID Required

Approver: OM/OD/HR

Requirement: Prior HR approval and official Solo Parent ID.

Bereavement Leave

Immediate Family Only

Approver: OM/OD/HR

Requirement: Documents provided to HR (regular employees).

Critical Note on Ticket Processing

Suspension tickets will be rejected if required approvals (OM/OD/HR) are missing, necessary documents like medical certificates are not attached, or the submission is past the deadline.
